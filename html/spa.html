Spa

In a quiet corner of the island, the Araamu Spa is a precious retreat within the resort. Put yourself into the sensitive hands of our experienced therapists and enjoy the sensual pleasure of a great massage. And there’sno need to be selfish about it,

Vasthi (Enema Therapy)

Ayurvedic Vasthi involves the introduction into the rectum, of herbal concoctions of sesame oil or medicated oil or ghee and herbal preparation in a liquid medium given as enema to clean the colon and increase the muscle tone. It is one of the effective treatments in Ayurveda

Open Hours | 1000 - 2200 Hrs | ARAAMU SPA

Bondithan

It is a highly rejuvenating treatment in which fresh herbal leaves are fried with several other herbal ingredients and tied in to boluses, dipped in warm medicated oil and massaged all over the body or in the area of the body that needs concentration,

Open Hours | 1000 - 2200 Hrs | ARAAMU SPA

Navarakizhi

This is a highly effective rejuvenation technique using a special type of rice – navara - cooked overnight in milk decoction, which is tied into boluses and again dipped into herbal decoction and warm milk,

Open Hours | 1000 - 2200 Hrs | ARAAMU SPA

Lymphatic

In this massage, you can feel a rhythmic series of strokes carried out in the direction of the venous and lymphatic flow using fingers and palms. The lymph system is very much taken in to account in Ayurveda massage.

Open Hours | 1000 - 2200 Hrs | ARAAMU SPA
